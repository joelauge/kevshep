About Vision Minute

See God. See others. See the future.

Welcome to Vision Minute — weekly reflections designed to anchor your leadership in faith, clarity, and purpose.

Each short message blends practical and spiritual principles to help you see God's hand at work, lead with integrity, and navigate the future with confidence.
